6 Enables a full trace dump of all incoming and outgoing data, including
7 descriptive information, to the given output file. Use "-" as filename to have
8 the output sent to stdout.
9 
10 This is very similar to --trace, but leaves out the hex part and only shows
11 the ASCII part of the dump. It makes smaller output that might be easier to
12 read for untrained humans.
