12 Curl is a command-line tool for transferring data specified with URL
13 syntax. Find out how to use curl by reading [the curl.1 man
14 page](https://curl.haxx.se/docs/manpage.html) or [the MANUAL
15 document](https://curl.haxx.se/docs/manual.html). Find out how to install Curl
16 by reading [the INSTALL document](https://curl.haxx.se/docs/install.html).
17 
18 libcurl is the library curl is using to do its job. It is readily available to
19 be used by your software. Read [the libcurl.3 man
20 page](https://curl.haxx.se/libcurl/c/libcurl.html) to learn how!
21 
22 You find answers to the most frequent questions we get in [the FAQ
23 document](https://curl.haxx.se/docs/faq.html).
24 
25 Study [the COPYING file](https://curl.haxx.se/docs/copyright.html) for
26 distribution terms and similar. If you distribute curl binaries or other
27 binaries that involve libcurl, you might enjoy [the LICENSE-MIXING
28 document](https://curl.haxx.se/legal/licmix.html).
