5 Make curl display transfer progress as a simple progress bar instead of the

7 
8 This progress bar draws a single line of '#' characters across the screen and
9 shows a percentage if the transfer size is known. For transfers without a
10 known size, there will be space ship (-=o=-) that moves back and forth but
11 only while data is being transferred, with a set of flying hash sign symbols on
